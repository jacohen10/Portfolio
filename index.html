  • Elevate

    Ruby on Rails meal plan app for students and restaurants. The app handles all the ordering and meal tracking logistics.

      Features include:

    • CanCanCan for authorization depending on the role of the user(customer, restaurant, admin)
    • Business hours so customers can only order from open restaurants
    • Mailers to notify restaurants of meals that were ordered ahead
    • Automated phone calls with the Twilio API as a 2nd alert for meals that were ordered in advance
